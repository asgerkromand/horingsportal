You will receive multiple consecutive images (pages) from a single document.

Classify the entire document using these labels:

Label 0: The document is NOT a response. Examples:
- A draft law, regulation, explanatory note, or technical annex.
- An invitation to give input (e.g., it shares a draft and asks for “bemærkninger” or feedback).
- An internal memo, administrative notice, or cover letter.
- It mentions “høring” or “høringssvar”, but is initiating or inviting a hearing — NOT responding to one.

Label 1: The document IS a formal response to a political consultation/hearing (a “høringssvar”).
Examples:
- It uses the term “høringssvar” or “høringsvar” and is framed as feedback.
- It contains comments, opinions, or input (e.g., “vi har ingen bemærkninger”).
- “Bemærkninger” is used to *give* feedback — not request it.
- The document clearly contributes to a hearing or consultation process.

Label 2: The content is illegible or too ambiguous to determine.

----------------------------------------
CRITICAL DISTINCTION:

⚠️ If the document ASKS FOR feedback → Label 0  
(e.g., “vi beder om bemærkninger” or “send svar senest…”)

✅ If the document GIVES feedback → Label 1  
(e.g., “vores bemærkninger er…” or “vi har ingen bemærkninger”)

Always classify based on the document’s OVERALL PURPOSE.
